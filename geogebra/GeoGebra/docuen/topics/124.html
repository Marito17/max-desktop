Continuity

GeoGebra allows you to turn the continuity heuristic On or Off in the Options menu. The software uses a near-to-heuristic to keep moving intersection points (e. g., line-conic, conic-conic) close to their old positions and avoid jumping intersection points.

 

Note: By default this heuristic is turned off. For user defined tools continuity is always turned off as well.
