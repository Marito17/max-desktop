LaTeX Formulas

In GeoGebra you can also write formulas. To do so, check the box LaTeX formula in the dialog of the  Text mode and enter your formula in LaTeX syntax.

Here, some important LaTeX commands are explained. Please have a look at any LaTeX documentation for further information.
