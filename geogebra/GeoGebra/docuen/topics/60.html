Tangents

Tangents to a conic can be produced in two ways.

·         Marking a point A and a conic c produces all tangents through A to c.

·         Marking a line g and a conic c produces all tangents to c that are parallel to g.

 

Marking a point A and a function f produces the tangent line to f in x = x(A).
