Move drawing pad

Drag and drop the drawing pad to move the origin of the coordinate system.

Note: You can also move the drawing pad by pressing the Shift key (PC: also Ctrl key) and dragging it with the mouse.

 

In this mode you can also scale each of the axes by dragging it with the mouse.

Note: Scaling the axes is also possible in every other mode by pressing and holding the Shift key (PC: also Ctrl key) while dragging the axis.
