General Notes

The algebraic representations of mathematical objects (e. g., values, coordinates, equations) are shown in the Algebra View. You can create and modify objects by using the Input Bar at the bottom of the GeoGebra window (see sections Direct Inputand and Commands).

Note: Always press the Enter-key after typing algebraic input into the Input Bar.

 

Note: Pressing the Enter-key at any time toggles the focus between the Input Bar and the Graphics View. This allows you to enter expressions and commands into the Input Bar without having to click on it with the mouse first.
