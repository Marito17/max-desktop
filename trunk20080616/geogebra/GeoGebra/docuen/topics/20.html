3.1.5.        Axes Ratio

Right click (MacOS: Apple + click) on the drawing pad and select Properties to get a context menu where you can

·         change the ratio between x-axis and y-axis

·         hide / show the coordinate axes individually

·         modify the layout of the axes (e.g. ticks, color, line style)
