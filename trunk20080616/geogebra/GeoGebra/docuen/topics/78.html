Angle

This mode creates …

·         angle between three points

·         angle between two segments

·         angle between two lines

·         angle between two vectors

·         all interior angles of a polygon

 

All these angles are limited in size between 0 and 180°. If you want to allow reflex angles, choose the appropriate setting in the Properties dialog.
