Welcome to Max 3.1 beta

Max is an operating system designed with education in mind. It is based on Ubuntu, a complete operating system that uses the Linux kernel and freely available to the public.

The Max project is built on the ideas enshrined in the Ubuntu philosophy: that software should be available free of charge, that software tools should be usable by people in their local language, and that people should have the freedom to customize and alter their software in whatever way they need.

Getting Help with Max:

There are many ways to get help with Max.

Access Max's guides and documentation by selecting "Help" from your System menu. You can also find help from the official Ubuntu documentation website and the community contributed documentation.

If you prefer some human contact, you can subscribe to the Max mailing list, web forums, or chat with the community on Freenode IRC Channel: #max.

You can also obtain commercial support.

Participate in Max:

The Max community is made up of students, teachers, software developers, documentation writers, translators, graphic artists, and most importantly, the people who are using Max in their everyday life. We invite you to join this community and help shape Max into an operating system that better meets your needs.

To find out how to participate, visit Max's community participation page!
